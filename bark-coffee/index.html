Fig 2. The namesake.

The Dog in the Bookshop

Bark Coffee borrows its name from Molly, the resident dog who keeps watch at the front of Alan Hanna's Bookshop. In 2013, two friends carved out this space at the back, marrying the scent of old paper with freshly extracted espresso.

@booksagusbeans

Our digital moniker. Agus is the Irish word for and. Books and beans; a quiet, perfect partnership.
