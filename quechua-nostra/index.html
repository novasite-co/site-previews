IDENTIDAD

Pride in Identity. Roots in Spanish Harlem.

"Quechua Nostra" translates to Our Quechua. It represents a rebirth, a reclamation of heritage, and an unwavering commitment to the authentic, uncompromising flavors of Peru.

Nestled in the heart of East Harlem, we aren't just a restaurant; we are a vibrant piece of the neighborhood's fabric. From the smoky allure of our signature Pollo a la Brasa to the explosive acidity of our Ceviches, every dish is an ode to the Andes and the coast.
