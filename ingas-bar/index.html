Named for Inga

Inga's Bar is named for chef Sean Rembold's great-aunt Inga, who hosted large family gatherings at her Louisville kitchen. Sean and his wife, the designer Caron Callahan, opened Inga's in 2022 in the old Jack the Horse space on Hicks and Cranberry — a corner of the Brooklyn Heights fruit streets that had quietly waited for its next chapter. The philosophy, as Sean tells it: simple, seasoned, and seasonal.
