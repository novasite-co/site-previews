Family-Run Since 2014.

Michael O'Donovan and family planted roots right on the platform edge. MIMA Coffee Company isn't just a clever location — it's a community anchor in Ranelagh, serving commuters, locals, and dog walkers beside Morton's Food Market.

Operating from a compact kiosk, the rhythm here is dictated by the pulse of the Green Line. Every five minutes, another tram empties out, bringing another wave of regulars.

The Irish Times Feb 2019

When a proposed Metro plan threatened to cut Ranelagh in half, Michael was one of the community voices who spoke up and helped push it back.
